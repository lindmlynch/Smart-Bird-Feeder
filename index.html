I have a bird feeder for small birds in my garden. I usually fill it in the morning before I leave for work but know very little about what goes on at the feeder when I am gone

I wanted to create a Smart Bird Feeder that would tell me more about the activities at the feeder. This was achieved using a RaspberryPi, PIR sensor, camera and some Python code.

It's important to remember to leave food out for the birds especially at this time of year. Using ThingSpeak and Open Weather API, a reminder tweet is sent when the temperature is below five degrees.

Platforms
