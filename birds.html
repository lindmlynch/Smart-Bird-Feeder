Who visits the Bird Feeder?

I was pleasantly surprised by the variety of birds visiting the feeder. These included: starlings, blue tits and even a robin. The camera captured mainly smaller garden birds with little sign of crows or magpies.

There was also more activity than anticipated with a steady stream of visitors, with any lulls seeming to coincide with bad weather conditions.

Set Up
Items Required
RPi, sensor and camera conected in a weather-proof case.
